About DoseMate

DoseMate is a comprehensive medication tracking app designed specifically for people using GLP-1 medications like Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, and Zepbound. Built by real users who understand the daily challenges of managing these medications, DoseMate helps you stay on track with your treatment plan.

Key Features

  • Track injection dates and medication schedules
  • Monitor weight progress with visual charts
  • Log and track side effects
  • Set customizable reminders for injections
  • Sync with Apple HealthKit
  • Support for multiple GLP-1 medications
  • Clean, intuitive interface designed for ease of use

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new medication?

Tap the "Medications" tab, then tap the "+" button in the top right corner. Enter your medication name, dosage, and schedule. DoseMate supports all major GLP-1 medications including Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, and Zepbound.

Can I track multiple medications?

Yes! DoseMate allows you to track multiple GLP-1 medications simultaneously. Each medication can have its own schedule, dosage, and reminder settings.

How do I set up reminders?

When adding or editing a medication, enable the "Reminders" toggle. You can customize the reminder time to fit your schedule. Make sure to allow notifications when prompted.

Does DoseMate sync with Apple Health?

Yes, DoseMate integrates with Apple HealthKit to sync your weight data. You can enable this in the app settings. Your medication data stays private within DoseMate.

How do I log a side effect?

Go to your medication details and tap "Log Side Effect." You can record the type, severity, and any notes about the side effect. This helps you and your healthcare provider monitor your treatment.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All your health data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. We do not collect or transmit your personal health information to any servers.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data to generate a report of your medication history, weight trends, and side effects. You can share this with your healthcare provider.

Troubleshooting

Reminders not working?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your iPhone Settings > Notifications > DoseMate. Also check that Do Not Disturb is not active during your reminder times.

HealthKit not syncing?

Go to iPhone Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > DoseMate and ensure all permissions are enabled. You may need to disconnect and reconnect the integration in DoseMate settings.

App crashing or freezing?

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, restart your iPhone. Make sure you're running the latest version of iOS and DoseMate.
